Salmon Poke Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sushi-grade salmon
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil, to taste
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar, to taste
  • 2 to 3 Tbsp liquid aminos, to taste
  • 1 to 3 tsp sriracha, to taste
  • 2 stalks green onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds
  • sea salt, to taste

Method

  1. Thaw sushi-grade salmon in your refrigerator until completely thawed. Place salmon on a clean cutting board on top of a clean counter top. Use a knife to remove the salmon skin.
  2. Chop the salmon into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Place salmon in a clean mixing bowl.
  4. Stir together the ingredients for the sauce in a measuring cup or bowl and pour over the chopped salmon. Stir well until everything is combined.
